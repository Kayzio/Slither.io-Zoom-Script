Sliter.IO Zoom Tool
==============================
When you are in game, hit the key <f12> to open the browser's inspect function. 
Click on the tab "Console." Here you will see a little arrow pointing to the right.
Drag the included js file where the pointing arrow is, and hit the <enter> key.
Using your mousewheel, you can zoom in/out inside your game!
